## BloomKnot - Personalized Wedding Planning Platform

BloomKnot is a comprehensive wedding planning platform that allows couples to create their dream wedding experience based on their unique preferences, budget, and style. The platform offers an intuitive interface for customizing wedding packages with various add-ons and services, while providing seamless communication with our wedding planning team.

## How It Works

### For Couples:

1. **Create Your Account**: Sign up with basic details about your wedding plans
2. **Build Your Package**: Start with a base package and customize according to your preferences:
   - Venue selection
   - Guest count & seating arrangements
   - Catering options
   - Decor themes & floral arrangements
   - Entertainment choices
   - Photography & videography services
   - Pre-wedding & post-wedding services
3. **Review Budget**: See real-time cost estimates as you customize your package
4. **Schedule Consultation**: Book a meeting with our wedding planners via Cal.com integration
5. **Finalize & Pay**: After consultation, confirm your selections and make payment
6. **Track Progress**: Monitor wedding preparation progress through your dashboard

### For Admin:

1. **Manage Bookings**: View and manage all upcoming wedding bookings
2. **Client Dashboard**: Track client preferences, budgets, and consultation schedules
3. **Vendor Coordination**: Assign and communicate with vendors for each event
4. **Timeline Management**: Create and update event timelines for each wedding
5. **Budget Tracking**: Monitor expenses and payments for each client
6. **Meeting Calendar**: View and prepare for upcoming client consultations
